New Land Sale Program Coming Soon!

Building on lessons learned from the Large Lots program, the Department of Planning and Development (DPD) is currently developing a brand new land sale program. DPD will engage with neighbors, community development organizations, City Council, and more to inform the program design, with a goal of opening land sale applications in late fall of 2022.

To stay up to date, please sign up for the DPD newsletter.
